Arul's Hobbies

My personal development and daily life are significantly shaped by a consistent engagement in several key hobbies: fitness training at the gym, swimming, video gaming, and reading. Each activity contributes distinctly to my physical and mental well-being, fostering discipline, strategic thinking, and continuous learning.

My commitment to fitness commenced two and a half years ago. This period marked a dedicated involvement in gym-based strength training. While a recent knee injury temporarily impacted my ability to perform exercises such as deadlifts and squats, I have since resumed deadlifting. A current personal best in the bench press is 245 pounds, indicative of consistent training effort.

Swimming has recently been integrated into my routine, primarily to support current weight management goals. This re-engagement follows a substantial prior history with the sport, having competed in high school and begun training at the age of five. This background provides a foundation for the current application of swimming as a complementary physical activity.

My engagement with video games includes Pokemon Legends: Arceus and Mario Kart 8. In Pokemon Legends: Arceus, my primary objective is the comprehensive completion of the Pokédex prior to progressing to the game's final stages. The game's open-world design and 3D Pokémon models are notable features that enhance this experience. Mario Kart 8 presents a different kind of challenge, focusing on the mastery of complex in-game shortcuts. The process involves identifying these shortcuts, extensive practice, and their subsequent successful implementation during competitive races.

Reading serves as a consistent activity providing mental engagement, with a current focus on The Art of War. This hobby facilitates exposure to diverse narratives and informational content, contributing to a broader understanding and continuous intellectual development.

Collectively, these hobbies represent a structured approach to personal enrichment. They provide avenues for physical exertion, strategic problem-solving, and intellectual growth, forming an integral part of my daily existence and ongoing self-improvement.
